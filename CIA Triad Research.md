# CIA Triad Research

## 1. Confidentiality

### Definition
Confidentiality means protecting information from unauthorized access.

### Why It Is Important
It ensures that sensitive data is only accessible to authorized users.

### Real-World Example
Online banking systems protect customer account details using encryption and secure authentication.

---

## 2. Integrity

### Definition
Integrity means ensuring that data remains accurate, complete, and unchanged unless modified by authorized individuals.

### Why It Is Important
It prevents unauthorized modifications and maintains trust in information.

### Real-World Example
University examination results should not be altered by unauthorized users.

---

## 3. Availability

### Definition
Availability means ensuring that systems and data are accessible whenever authorized users need them.

### Why It Is Important
Organizations depend on continuous access to services and information.

### Real-World Example
A college website should remain accessible for students during admissions and examinations.

---

| Principle | Definition | Importance | Example |
|------------|------------|------------|---------|
