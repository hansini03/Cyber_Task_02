# Cyber Attack Case Study: WannaCry Ransomware

## What Happened?

WannaCry was a ransomware attack that spread worldwide in May 2017. It infected thousands of computers and encrypted files, demanding payment in Bitcoin to restore access.

## Which Part of the CIA Triad Was Affected?

### Availability
Users could not access their files and systems.

### Integrity
Files were modified and encrypted without permission.

## Impact

- More than 200,000 computers were affected.
- Hospitals, businesses, and government organizations faced disruptions.
- Financial losses reached millions of dollars.

## Prevention Methods

- Regular system updates and patch management.
- Frequent data backups.
- Employee cybersecurity awareness training.
- Use of antivirus and endpoint protection solutions.

## Conclusion

The WannaCry attack demonstrated the importance of maintaining updated systems and strong cybersecurity practices.
